History

In brief of History of

Golden Mountain

Founded in 1985, Adventure Rivers Rafting Company began as a small family business in the Appalachian region, offering thrilling whitewater rafting experiences. Starting with just a few rafts and passionate guides, the company quickly gained a reputation for safety and customer satisfaction. By the mid-1990s, Adventure Rivers expanded its offerings to include more challenging routes and eco-tourism initiatives, partnering with local organizations to promote river conservation. As demand grew, the company ventured beyond the Appalachian region, introducing trips on iconic rivers like the Colorado and Salmon. Today, Adventure Rivers is a leading outfitter, known for its exciting adventures, expert guides, and commitment to environmental responsibility, inspiring adventure seekers of all ages.
